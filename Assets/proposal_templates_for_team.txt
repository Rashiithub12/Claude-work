SMART QUESTIONS BY JOB TYPE (add one to your proposal)
================================================================================

DATA ANNOTATION:
- Do you have annotation guidelines ready or should I follow standard practices?
- What format do you need for the final output?
- Is there a specific tool you want me to use?
- What is the expected accuracy threshold?

VIRTUAL ASSISTANT:
- What timezone are you in?
- Do you prefer email, chat, or calls?
- What tools or software are you currently using?
- What are your expected response times?

WEB RESEARCH:
- What specific fields do you need for each entry?
- Do you have preferred sources?
- How should I handle entries with missing information?
- Do you need verification notes?

DATA ENTRY:
- What is the source format?
- Are there specific formatting rules?
- What is the expected daily volume?
- Should I flag unclear entries or use my judgment?


================================================================================
THINGS TO NEVER SAY
================================================================================

- "Dear Sir/Madam" or "Dear Hiring Manager"
- "I hope this message finds you well"
- "I am a hardworking professional"
- "I am the best candidate for this job"
- "I am highly motivated and passionate"
- "Please give me a chance"
- Any form of begging or desperation
- Generic phrases that could apply to any job
